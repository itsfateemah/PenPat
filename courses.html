Cloud Security

The Cloud Security Course delves into the specialized techniques and principles essential for safeguarding data, applications, and infrastructure hosted in cloud environments. Students explore topics such as identity and access management, encryption, network security, and compliance requirements specific to cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud. By mastering cloud security best practices and tools, students acquire the expertise needed to mitigate risks, protect against cyber threats, and ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of cloud-based assets.
